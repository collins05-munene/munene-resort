African Cuisines

Indulge in authentic African flavors from various regions.

Jollof Rice (Nigeria)

Aromatic rice dish with tomato sauce, spices, and grilled chicken or fish.

Bunny Chow (South Africa)

Delicious curry served in a hollowed-out bread loaf. A must-try comfort food.

Injera (Ethiopia)

Soft sour flatbread served with spicy stews and vegetables.

Couscous (Morocco)

Fluffy semolina dish served with vegetables, lamb, or chicken.

Piri Piri Chicken (Mozambique)

Grilled chicken marinated in spicy piri piri sauce, served with sides.

Sadza (Zimbabwe)

Starchy cornmeal dish served with hearty stews or vegetables.

Brochettes (Rwanda)

Grilled skewers of marinated meat, usually served with a side of fries.
